Summary of current status

• VimConf has been running since 2013 as an international Vim conference
• There's no VimConf 2020. It was been cancelled due to COVID-19. We weren't ready to hold an alternative official online event.
• Another new conference, vimconf.live by @ABD, has been successfully done in 2020 instead.
• There's no set plans for VimConf 2021 yet.
• VimConf 2021 venue in Akihabara, Tokyo can be available, but its after party won't be available.
• I think either VimConf 2021 can merge vimconf.live staff, vimconf.live staff can merge VimConf 2021 staff, or merge both events into one

I think its appropriate for everyone to have their opinion and be able to express it, however I dont think its appropriate for people to present one idea for their presentation (which is what we recieved) and then to, suddenly like switch their presentation and present something else) which is what happened. 3. We plan to adopt a speaker code of conduct and lecture guidelines which all speakers will need to accept and to provide us with their slides and presentation before the conference in order for us to see / approve.
4. If something would happen again (That after a speaker agrees to our T&Cs for speakers and presents to us their presentation) and they then live decide to present something else, we would likely pause and explain to the audience that whats being shown is according to the original plan and we need to address it
