ド・モルガンの法則

not (a and b) = (not a) or  (not b)
not (a or  b) = (not a) and (not b)

(a nand a) nand (b nand b)
= not (a and a) nand not (b and b)
= not a nand not b
= not (not a and not b)
= (not not a) or (not not b)
= a or b
